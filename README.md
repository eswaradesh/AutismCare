AuCare is a comprehensive digital platform designed to foster seamless collaboration between parents of children with autism and their therapists. It empowers caregivers to track daily routines and behaviors, while providing therapists with data-driven, non-diagnostic AI insights to optimize support strategies.

## 🚀 Vision

To bridge the communication gap in autism care through real-time data sharing, AI-powered behavioral analytics, and secure collaboration tools.

---

## ✨ Key Platform Features

### 👨‍👩‍👧 For Parents
- **Daily Tracking**: Log routines (sleep, food, therapy) and behavioral entries with ease.
- **Therapist Management**: Search for verified therapists and securely invite them to collaborate.
- **Granular Consent**: Total control over what data is shared (routines, behaviors, medications, etc.).
- **Automatic Alerts**: Shared behavior alerts for sudden changes that need attention.

### 👨‍⚕️ For Therapists
- **Patient Dashboard**: Unified view of all children under care with "Needs Attention" status indicators.
- **Behavioral Timeline**: In-depth analysis of behavior intensity and frequency trends.
- **AI-Powered Insights**: Behavioral intelligence that identifies patterns, baselines, and deviations.
- **Observational Notes**: Securely document professional observations without altering parent-logged data.

### 🛡️ For Admins
- **Verification System**: Rigorous multi-level verification process for healthcare professionals.
- **Account Management**: Ability to approve, reject, or suspend therapist accounts to ensure platform safety.
